Delivering Precision in Every Component Since 2014

Star Industries, established in 2014 in Chakan, Pune, is a trusted manufacturer of precision sheet metal press components and machined components. We specialize in producing high-quality parts for ferrous and non-ferrous metals, serving diverse industrial sectors with reliability and consistency. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we maintain strict quality control at every stage from design and prototyping to production and delivery. Our advanced infrastructure, skilled workforce, and customer-focused approach help us deliver excellence with every product.
