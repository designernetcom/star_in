Our History

How we started

2014

Foundation Year

Star Industries was launched in the Chakan–Pune industrial hub with a small but skilled technical team. The mission? Bring precision + quality together in sheet metal press and machined components

2015

First Production Line Ready

Full-scale production kicked off. The company started taking on early clients and delivering consistent metal press components with tight tolerances.

2016

Expansion of Machine Capabilities

New machinery and upgraded infrastructure were added, helping Star Industries boost output and speed without compromising quality.

2021

Stronger Client Base

Secured long-term partnerships with major OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers owing to precision, on-time delivery, and custom engineering capabilities.

2024

10 Years of Excellence

Celebrated a decade of delivering top-notch Sheet Metal Press and Machined Components backed by innovation, customer-first focus, and quality commitment.
