Our History

Manufacturing Evolution & Capability Enhancement

2014
Company established with basic press infrastructure, focusing on controlled manufacturing and disciplined processes.
2018
Introduction of pneumatic press technology to enhance process consistency, repeatability, and operator safety.
2022
Commissioning of a 10,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility enabling improved layout, material flow, and quality compliance.
2024
Press capacity enhanced up to 150 Tons, supporting higher-load components and customer-specific requirements.
2025
Complete migration to pneumatic press machines aligned with automotive standards for safety, precision, and productivity.
