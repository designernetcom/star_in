About Star

Star Industries- Precision in Every Component

Star Industries is a leading manufacturer of high-precision Sheet Metal Press and Machined Components

Founded in 2014, Star Industries is a trusted manufacturer of Sheet Metal Press and Machined Components, driven by a skilled technical team committed to precision and quality. Based in the Chakan–Pune Industrial Area, our ISO 9001:2015 certified facility uses advanced technology to deliver reliable, high-quality products. We aim to be a leading supplier through continuous innovation and customer satisfaction.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified for consistent quality and reliability.
  • Experts in Sheet Metal Press and Machined Components
  • Committed to innovation, on-time delivery, customer satisfaction.
