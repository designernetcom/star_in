Precision CNC Wirecut EDM for Complex & High-Accuracy Components

At Star Industries, our CNC Wirecut EDM division specializes in manufacturing intricate and high-precision components with exceptional accuracy and consistency. Utilizing advanced CNC wire electrical discharge machining technology, we deliver solutions for complex geometries, tight tolerances, and hardened materials that demand superior precision.

Our Wirecut EDM capabilities enable burr-free machining, sharp internal corners, and micron-level accuracy without inducing mechanical stress or distortion. Through optimized cutting parameters, precision-controlled spark erosion, and strict process monitoring, we ensure outstanding surface finish, dimensional stability, and repeatability.

From tool and die manufacturing to precision industrial components, our CNC Wirecut EDM processes support both prototype and high-volume production. Every component is subjected to rigorous inspection and quality assurance, ensuring complete compliance with customer specifications and industry standards.
